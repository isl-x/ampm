Peter LaMotte
Peter LaMotte oversees the digital practice at LEVICK where he services their global client base of corporations, associations, governments, and non-profits. Mr. LaMotte focuses on helping them prepare, survive, and recover from reputational threats and crises all while protecting their brand equity. A veteran of digital marketing and online advertising, Mr. LaMotte has spent the majority of his career helping organizations better understand how managing their reputation both on and offline can be the foundation for a successful enterprise. He is regularly quoted in the Washington Post, The Wall Street Journal, and The New York Times. He has taught on the topic of cyber-security, crisis management, online marketing, and digital reputation at George Washington University, University of Maryland, and American University. Prior to LEVICK, Mr. LaMotte was CEO of the online marketing and advertising agency GeniusRocket where he created the first advertising agency powered by a distributed global workforce. He has also worked at IBM, Apple, FedEx, Blackbaud, and the Marketing Leadership Council. He has a Masters in Business Administration from Vanderbilt University.
