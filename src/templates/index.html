Adam Zuckerman, Director of Ventures & Innovation at Discovery Communications
OUR NEXT PM
Adam Zuckerman

Leads the strategy behind rapid identification, vetting, and implementation of current and emerging technologies. He is responsible for interfacing and partnering with innovators who exhibit the potential to impact Discovery's operations, while also introducing the global workforce to cutting edge technologies and tools in the industry.

Adam created and maintains Discovery’s Consumer Device Lab. This lab educates employees worldwide about future-facing technologies, which have the potential to impact Discovery’s numerous offerings.

Additionally, Adam founded Fosterly, an organization which empowers the startup ecosystem in the greater DC area. He has also served on advisory boards of several organizations including the DocuSign Customer Council and Box.com’s Product Advisory Board. He also mentors entrepreneurs and startups, and provides commentary on air for CNBC.com.
